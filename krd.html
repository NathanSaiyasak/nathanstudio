Krd.

Simplifying Dining Choices

Overview

Project Type: UX/UI Design, Branding, Product Design

Role: UX/UI Designer, Brand Strategist, Visual Designer

Tools Used: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, Product Prototyping

Krd. was designed to make restaurant decisions easier. Instead of endless scrolling, users receive a lightweight, randomized suggestion based on location and preference.

The Challenge

As a solo designer, the challenge was balancing product clarity, brand consistency, and fast execution. The interface had to stay simple while still feeling distinctive and engaging.

My Approach

I built the visual identity and UX together from the start. Through iterative wireframes, quick testing, and focused refinement, the product evolved into a cohesive system across app, posters, and social assets.

What I Accomplished

  • Designed a clean interface that improves decision speed and usability.
  • Created a cohesive brand system for digital and print touchpoints.
  • Launched core product screens and supporting campaign assets.
  • Managed design execution end-to-end as a solo contributor.
