Edmerse Web Development

Edmerse is an educational consultancy focused on connecting students with life-changing opportunities.

Overview

Project Type: UX/UI Design, Web Development, Branding

Role: UX/UI Designer, Web Developer, Brand Strategist

Tools Used: Figma, Adobe Illustrator, HTML, CSS, JavaScript

Edmerse is an educational platform designed to help students navigate study abroad opportunities with confidence. The experience was structured to reduce confusion and simplify decision-making.

The Challenge

Navigating study abroad can feel overwhelming due to fragmented information and unclear application pathways. Students needed a clear user journey with practical steps and better access to reliable insights.

My Approach

I conducted user research and mapped core pain points before wireframing a cleaner, clearer flow. Through iterative prototyping and testing, the product evolved into a responsive platform that improves clarity and engagement.

What I Accomplished

The final result was a structured digital experience that streamlines the study abroad process. The platform presents guidance in digestible steps and helps students make informed choices faster.
